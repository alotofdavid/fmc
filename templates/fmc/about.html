What is the Weekly FMC?

The Weekly Fewest Moves Challenge (FMC) was made for the SpeedSolving.com web forums so that fewest moves enthusiasts can regularly compete with others and share solutions to the same scramble in order to learn from each other.

Although it is not required to submit a solution, creating an account allows you to reserve a name. Creating accounts will also create a profile for you that lists all of your submissions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FMC?

FMC stands for Fewest Moves Challenge. The challenge is to find an algorithm that will solve the cube based on the scramble provided for you.
